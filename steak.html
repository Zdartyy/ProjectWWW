Grilled Steak with Vegetables

Description

Our grilled steak is cooked to perfection and served with a side of roasted vegetables. A hearty and satisfying meal for any meat lover.

Ingredients

Price

Taste

Our grilled steak is juicy and flavorful, with a perfect char on the outside and tender meat on the inside. The roasted vegetables add a delicious and healthy side.

Pair this steak with a glass of red wine, such as Cabernet Sauvignon or Merlot, and a side of mashed potatoes or a fresh garden salad.
