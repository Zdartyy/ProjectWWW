Risotto with Tiger Prawns

Description

This risotto is a creamy and delicious dish made with Arborio rice, tiger prawns, and a rich seafood broth. It's perfect for a special dinner or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Tiger prawns
  • Seafood broth
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • White wine
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Price

$18.99

Taste

This risotto has a rich and creamy texture with a delightful seafood flavor. The tiger prawns add a succulent and slightly sweet taste, perfectly complemented by the savory Parmesan cheese and aromatic herbs.

Serving Suggestions

Pair this risotto with a crisp white wine, such as Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Grigio, to enhance the seafood flavors. Serve with a side of fresh garden salad for a complete meal.
