Creamy Pasta with Mushrooms

Description

This creamy pasta dish is made with tender mushrooms and a rich, velvety sauce. It's a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Mushrooms
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Price

$14.99

Taste

This pasta has a rich and creamy texture with earthy mushroom flavors. The Parmesan cheese adds a savory depth, making it a delightful dish.

Serving Suggestions

Pair this pasta with a glass of Chardonnay or Pinot Noir. Serve with a side of garlic bread and a fresh green salad for a complete meal.
